Hello and welcome to the website for Aaron Filson Ceramics. My artistic journey has been straight forward in some ways, and in others not so much. For more information on my background, read the Bio I have written for myself. You may be interested in learning more about the projects I have going. Check the progress here. There are a number of terms that are used by potters and ceramic artists, and which are not in common usage. I have written a short page of terms, explained in a way that you will better understand the rest of my website, and perhaps the ceramics world in general.

Perhaps my favorite part of the website is the gallery. I have a small collection of photographs posted there to show my prior work and art. The progress in my artistic sensibilities and thematic choices is highlighted in this gallery section.

A small page of contact info and other links are here at the contact page. At this point it is easiest to use the Facebook interface to contact me but I know that there are real people who are on the internet and not on Facebook.

A while ago, I changed hosting providers, and this website disappeared from the web. Now back, and newly improved, the website should give you a window into what I am working on, and some of the projects of the past.
