The projects I have worked on- mostly kilns.

  • A small project to house the propane updraft kiln at my studio in Tacoma.
  • The project to build and operate a soda kiln. We have salted the kiln, which means it will forever be a salt/Soda kiln. A wide range of results have been delivered into my hands from this project. Expensive failures, dull and bland surfaces, falling flaming doors, and ruining pieces have mostly changed over to exciting textures and dramatic flashes of color. It continues to suffer from being too tall.
